🔒 Privacy & Data

Data & Privacy

How Batchly accesses, uses, and protects your data. No jargon.

What we access

When you connect your Petpooja account to Batchly, we access:

We access

  • ✓ Your daily sales history
  • ✓ Your menu items and categories
  • ✓ Your stock levels (closing stock)

We never access

  • ✗ Customer personal data or phone numbers
  • ✗ Payment or billing information
  • ✗ Staff or HR data
  • ✗ Any data outside your Petpooja sales records

How we use your data

Your sales data is used exclusively to:

  • Detect patterns in what sells and when
  • Generate your daily prep tips
  • Calculate your weekly savings
We do not use your data for advertising, benchmarking against other businesses, or any purpose other than generating your tips.

How we protect it

  • All Petpooja credentials are encrypted using AES-128 encryption before storage
  • Your data is stored on Railway's secure cloud infrastructure
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties
  • Access to your data is restricted to Batchly's systems only

Your rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Ask us to delete your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect your Petpooja account at any time from the app settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at admin@platbase.in or WhatsApp us at +91 73566 10002
